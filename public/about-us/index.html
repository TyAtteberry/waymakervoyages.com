Who We Are

At WayMaker Voyages, we are passionate about crafting unforgettable sailing experiences in the British Virgin Islands. Founded by seasoned sailors and travel enthusiasts, we understand that the best journeys are those where every detail is thoughtfully planned, allowing you to focus entirely on making memories.

Our mission is to remove the complexities of travel planning while preserving the sense of adventure. We specialize in designing bespoke itineraries that go beyond typical tourist experiences, tailoring each voyage to your family's unique interests and travel style.

Our Philosophy

We believe that travel should transform you. That's why we don't just arrange trips—we create meaningful experiences that connect families, foster personal growth, and deepen relationships. Our approach combines expert logistical planning with genuine care for each client's journey.
